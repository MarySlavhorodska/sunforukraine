We are SUNFORUKRAINE

Volunteers team based in Cracow

Sun for Ukraine - An organization of pro-Ukrainian activists in Krakow, which was created after the full-scale invasion of Russia in Ukraine, in order to conduct an information war, broadcast the position of Ukrainians abroad and help with the purchase of the necessary ammunition for the military, which protects the independence and integrity of the state sovereignty of Ukraine. We start new fundraiser for Ukrainian defenders. Thanks to this, they will not go on reconnaissance at their own risk, but will be able to effectively destroy the enemy. We ask each of you to contribute to the collection, because even a small amount can give a chance for life. Your one undrank coffee, can bring our victory closer.

Our history

The Sun for Ukraine team originates from the protests near the monument of Adam Mickiewicz, because it was there where most of our team members have met starting to do joint business. Initially, the main goal of the team was information warfare, namely, to make it so that every resident of Europe and the whole world would knew exactly what is happening in Ukraine, that is why "Sun" looked for and implemented into reality any ways of helping Ukraine. At first it was quite not decisive and with some mistakes, but then with more and more persistence. After several months, it became clear to the team that the information war is not enough and the Ukrainian army must be helped directly. Thus, "Sun" together with other participants of the protest began to organize concerts, exhibitions at which fundraisings were organised. In general, these actions defined one of the most basic goals of the organization in the direction of which we are moving up now. As of today, our team has slightly increased its focus on the organization of events such as: master classes, exhibitions of posters and paintings, concerts, etc. and disconnected from protests.

Our team

Our team consists of many people of completely different occupations who did not have much experience of organization of any events before the full-scale invasion.

"Sun" has a majority of Ukrainians and Poles, who are also present students. Therefore, very often they have to combine everything at once:
both study and work, and volunteering in the organization.

Founders

Tetyana Holiuk is a Ukrainian who has been living in Krakow for several years and currently studying for a master's degree at the Jagiellonian University. Tetiana is a great patriot, therefore, immediately after the news of relatives about a full-scale invasion of Ukraine she went to the border, where she volunteered for more than a month and at the same time counseled refugees on such questions as: "I'm in Poland, what to do next?", "How to legalize my stay?", "How to get a job?" because Tetyana herself graduated from the bachelor's degree in the specialty "International migration". Very often, the girl could help people without a break within 36 hours. Tatyana is very dedicated and hardworking, so she often volunteered getting the harm to her health. After returning to Krakow, the girl continued to look for some pro-Ukrainian initiatives, and over the years decided to create her own.
